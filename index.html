Monedo.com is a young, technology company

which delivers a range of complimentary and exciting financial services products to customers across Europe and beyond.

We are

the new kids on the block and we believe in doing things differently. Digital finance is our specialism which means we are 100% online. We’re there whenever the customer needs us.

We believe in making money as uncomplicated as possible, and we actively seek solutions to make our customer’s lives easier.

Monedo encompasses several fabulous brands

which offer complimentary services.

  • Monedo Now

    Short and medium term loans configured to work just for you.

    Tell us the amount and the repayment period and we’ll work up a bespoke finance package just for you.

  • Monedo Flex

    Monedo Flex delivers a line of credit which is available when you need it.

    You have a pre-agreed amount available to you but you only pay interest on what you draw down.

  • Monedo Pay

    Your ultimate digital wallet.

    Monedo Pay is designed for today’s fast-moving lifestyles and offers a free Virtual Payment Card as well as an optional MasterCard Prepaid Card.

  • Monedo Me

    A powerful, digital dashboard giving unrivalled access to real time, financial overviews.

    Monitor and improve credit scoring, create peer comparisons and use your past history to improve your future financial health.
